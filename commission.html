Commission a Portrait – What is Involved


The Process, Pricing, & Delivery


I work from photographs rather than from life - making commissioning a portrait easy for busy families or professionals. If you’ve looked through my portfolio and like my style, simply use the contact form to get in touch about your idea. From there, the process is straightforward:

1) Share photos & ideas

Send a few clear, well-lit favourites (no filters). Together, we’ll decide on the best reference, crop and background.

2) Confirm quote & booking

I’ll confirm price and timeline. A 10% deposit secures your commission and your place in my schedule.

3) Painting phase

I typically begin with a tonal underpainting, then build colour in layers. I can share a progress photo part-way through.

4) Approval & delivery

You’ll see photos of the finished piece for approval. The balance is due on approval, then your painting is shipped (UK-wide) or will be available for collection.


Prices (Guide)

Pricing depends on size and complexity (e.g. extra subjects, detailed backgrounds, etc.)

Oil on Canvas

  • A5 (5.8 x 8.3 inches) — from £300
  • A4 (8.3 x 11.7 inches) — from £500
  • A3 (11.7 x 16.5 inches) — from £750
  • Booking fee: 10% non-refundable deposit to secure your slot; remaining fee due on approval of the finished piece before shipping/collection.
  • Shipping: UK only, tracked. Collection is available for those based nearby.
  • Framing: Supplied unframed.

Timing

Typical turnaround is 4–8 weeks depending on size, complexity and drying time for oils. If you have a date in mind (birthday, anniversary, Christmas), let me know as early as you can — I’ll confirm what’s realistic and whether a painting is possible.
